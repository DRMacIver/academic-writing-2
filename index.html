three features of writing

pointwhat does your writing say?
flowin what order does it say it?
voice how does it say it?

how to write badly

pointunclear writing, digressions
flowrambling prose, hard to keep track
voicestilted or messy text

rules of point

  1. decide what your point is.
  2. say it.
  3. don't say things that are not your point.

rules of flow

  1. introduce things before you use them.
  2. connect things to what came before.
  3. keep related things near to each other.

rules of voice

  1. write as you want to be read.
  2. find a consistent tone.
  3. find a pace that guides the reader.
